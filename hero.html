Climate Change: The Facts

What Is Climate Change?

Climate change is long term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts can be natural, but human activities - especially the burning of fossil fuels - are now the main reasons for climate change.

Why It Matters

Rising temperatures can cause sea levels to rise, ecosystems to collapse, and extreme weather to intensify. This can make food security, public health, and the global economy get damaged.

What You Can Do

  • Use public transit, bike, or even walk when possible
  • Reduce meat and dairy consumption when possible
  • Turn off the tap when brushing teeth to conserve water
  • Turn off your lights, electronics, and more when your out of the house
  • Support renewable energy projects and get cleaner products
  • Demand action from elected officials and protest without worry
